To give you an overview, stress today primarily stems from areas such as job-related issues, economic hardship, health concerns, relationship problems, and the challenge of balancing work and family. Technological stress is also a prevalent factor, fuelling anxiety in this age of a constantly buzzing news cycle and social media.
